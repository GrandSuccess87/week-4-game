Crystals Collector

How to Play the Game

Each game a number is randomly generated and will be displayed in the seagreen box.

At the bottom of the screen are four crystals and each crystal holds a specific value.

The value of each crystal is hidden from you.

By clicking on each crystal you will add a specific amount of points to your total score

Your goal is to match your total score to the randomly generated number.

You win the game by matching your total score to the random number.

If your total score goes above the random number, you lose the game.

Each time the game starts, the values of the crystals change as well as the random number.
